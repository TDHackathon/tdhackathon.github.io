FAQs

Q: Who can come?

A: As long as you're a University of Waterloo student, feel free to signup!

Q: How much is this going to cost?

A: It's going to be absolutely free! Food will be provided.

Q: How big can teams be?

A: Teams must be either 3 or 4 people, with at least one female per team.

Q: Where is this happening?

A: We'll be in Mathematics 3 1006.

Q: What should I bring?

A: Whatever you need to hack (laptop, mouse, charger, etc.)! Feel free to bring a mug for coffee and snacks if you need that extra food.
