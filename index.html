Q: When do I need to register by?

A: 11:59 PM on May 20.

Q: How much is this going to cost?

A: Nothing at all. We'll be providing food and drinks throughout the event.

Q: How big can teams be?

A: Teams must be either 3 or 4 people.

Q: What should I bring?

A: Whatever you need to hack (laptop, mouse, charger, etc). Extra snacks and a water bottle aren't a half-bad idea either.

Q: Will there be hardware?

A: Unfortunately not, but you are allowed to hack on hardware and be eligible for the prizes nevertheless.
